Authentic. Healthy. Gourmet.

Welcome to Moh Mishti, the intersection of authenticity and innovation. We are all about harnessing heritage recipes to make accessible, premium, ready-to-eat treats, for both the health-conscious foodie and the nostalgic South-Asian alike. Founded by a passionate entrepreneur raised in Kolkata and rooted in Chicago, our mission is to share the inherent wholesomeness and lesser-known flavors of India with a modern twist.
