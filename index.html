Authentic. Healthy. Gourmet.

Welcome to Moh Mishti, the intersection of authenticity and innovation. We are all about harnessing heritage recipes to make accessible, premium, ready-to-eat treats. Founded by a passionate entrepreneur raised in Kolkata and rooted in Chicago, our mission is to share the inherent wholesomeness and lesser-known flavors of India with a modern twist.

Moh (मोह)

Moh means love, longing, and deep emotional connection. At Moh Mishti, it’s also our connection to time-honored fermentation—transforming milk into Doi (yogurt) using anti-inflammatory saffron and mineral-rich jaggery, that's as good for your gut as it is for your heart. Each jar of Moh Mishti Doi is a balance delicious nourishment, ancestral wisdom, and modern wellness.
