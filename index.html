Welcome to Moh Mishti, the intersection of authenticity and innovation. We are all about harnessing heritage recipes to make accessible, premium, ready-to-eat treats, for both the health-conscious foodie and the nostalgic South-Asian alike.

Our Mission

Bringing heritage to your table

Founded by a passionate entrepreneur raised in Kolkata and rooted in Chicago, our mission is to share the inherent wholesomeness and lesser-known flavors of India with a modern twist.

Moh Mishti Doi

Caramelized yogurt with saffron and jaggery

Our signature Mishti Doi is a traditional Bengali delicacy - a sweet, caramelized yogurt that's been perfected over generations. Made with premium ingredients including aromatic saffron and natural jaggery, it's a time-honored treat that's both indulgent and gut-healing.
